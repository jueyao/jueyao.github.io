Skincare

An analysis into the data from Skincarisma, a website compiling ingredients lists and other info about skincare products, to gain a deeper insight into the effects of the most popular ingredients. It will help affirm (or not) the beliefs that the ingredients of skincare products matter the most, and whether popular ingredients are as effective as many believe them to be.

Airbnb and Rent

Analyzing both Airbnb and housing market data can help give a deeper understanding to whether Airbnb's popularity in the lodging industry has affected real estate market performance in major U.S. cities. Key indicators of housing market performance include rent price, location of listing, count of listings, and date of listing.

Confidence Intervals

It is well-documented that the 95% confidence intervals for one and two sample tests derived from Wald tests have poor performance in terms of coverage rates, with the actual coverage probability being much lower than intended. The purpose of this project was to replicate and expand the findings of the above research. In addition, to investigate the performance of the adjusted Plus-4 intervals at other common levels of significance.

Major League Soccer

Major League Soccer is the top tier of American professional soccer, bringing in players from all around the world. Often, when a world class player finds the competition in the European leagues to be too tough, they will “retire” to the MLS. These players can command exceptionally large salaries compared to homegrown players in MLS. This causes a few exceptional outliers in the MLS salary distribution. With this in mind, the salaries in the MLS are interesting to compare. What exactly motivates a team to pay a certain player more?
